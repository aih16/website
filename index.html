I'm Aidan.

I am a Senior Developer at the Senedd, where I specialize as a "full-stack" engineer. My daily focus involves architecting and building internal (and the occasional external) web applications using the ASP.NET ecosystem to support the work of the Welsh Parliament.
My technical roots are in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, having earned both my Undergraduate and Postgraduate degrees in this field, with a side of robotics.

I've a keen interest in AI and Machine Learning ever since reading i,Robot in my first year of University, where I promptly changed my course from Video Game Development to AI & Robotics.

Specifically I'm interested in the field of Natural Language Processing, or NLP, as evidenced by my BSc and MSc dissertations revolving around sentiment analysis, extraction, classification and prediction based on text.

In my spare time I am also part of the Army Reserve, I have been a Reservist since 2020 when I joined the Royal Welsh regiment where I currently serve as an Infantryman in C Company, 3rd Battalion.
